Performance Protocol

  • Introduce the app and invite participants to join via their smartphones.
  • Instruct them to enter a valid 5 to 10 digit number. Suggest a random phone number with area code for 10 digits.
  • Advise them to use various digits rather than repeating the same one.
  • Have them manually scramble those digits in any order they wish.
  • The audience app will subtract the smaller resulting number from the larger one.
  • Ask the participant to select any non-zero digit from the result to keep as their secret number.

The Secret Method

Close your eyes and rub your forehead to focus. Mentally add each number they read together.

The Rule of Nine: While adding, never count the digit 9. Find the closest multiple of 9 that is below your total sum. Subtract the difference from 9 to reveal their secret number.

Example 1
Original: 9876543 | Scrambled: 3456789
Subtracted Result: 6419754
If they chose 4, they read: 6, 4, 1, 9, 7, 5.
6 + 4 + 1 + 7 + 5 = 23 (Ignore the 9)
Closest '9' below 23 is 18. Difference is 5.
9 - 5 = 4[cite: 33].
Example 2
Original: 73915 | Scrambled: 19537
Subtracted Result: 54378

4 + 3 + 7 + 8 = 22
Closest '9' below 22 is 18. Difference is 4.
9 - 4 = 5[cite: 80].
