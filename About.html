The Mind Reader

Instruction Manual & Examples

Performance Protocol

  • Introduce the app and invite participants to join via their smartphones[cite: 7].
  • Instruct them to enter a valid 5 to 10 digit number[cite: 8, 13]. Suggest a random phone number with area code for 10 digits[cite: 10].
  • Advise them to use various digits rather than repeating the same one[cite: 14].
  • Have them manually scramble those digits in any order they wish[cite: 15].
  • The audience app will subtract the smaller resulting number from the larger one[cite: 16].
  • Ask the participant to select any non-zero digit from the result to keep as their secret number[cite: 17, 18].
  • Have them read the remaining digits to you slowly[cite: 19, 20].

The Secret Method

Close your eyes and rub your forehead to focus. Mentally add each number they read together[cite: 22].

The Rule of Nine: While adding, never count the digit 9[cite: 32]. Find the closest multiple of 9 that is below your total sum. Subtract the difference from 9 to reveal their secret number[cite: 33, 35].

Example 1
Original: 9876543 | Scrambled: 3456789
Subtracted Result: 6419754
If they chose 4, they read: 6, 4, 1, 9, 7, 5.
6 + 4 + 1 + 7 + 5 = 23 (Ignore the 9)
Closest '9' below 23 is 18. Difference is 5.
9 - 5 = 4[cite: 33].
Example 2
Original: 73915 | Scrambled: 19537
Subtracted Result: 54378
If they chose 5, they read: 4, 3, 7, 8.
4 + 3 + 7 + 8 = 22
Closest '9' below 22 is 18. Difference is 4.
9 - 4 = 5[cite: 80].
